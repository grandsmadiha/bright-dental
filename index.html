FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and procedures.

How often should I visit the dentist?

We recommend visiting the dentist every 6 months for routine check-ups and professional cleanings. However, some patients may need more frequent visits depending on their oral health needs.

Is teeth whitening safe?

Yes, professional teeth whitening is completely safe when performed by trained dental professionals. We use FDA-approved products and monitor the entire process to ensure your safety and comfort.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major dental insurance plans. We also offer flexible payment plans and financing options to make quality dental care accessible to everyone.

How long do dental implants last?

With proper care and maintenance, dental implants can last a lifetime. The titanium implant post integrates with your jawbone, providing a permanent foundation. The crown may need replacement after 10-15 years due to normal wear.

What should I do in a dental emergency?

Contact our office immediately. We offer same-day emergency appointments and 24/7 phone support. For severe injuries or uncontrolled bleeding, go to the nearest emergency room first, then call us.
